So I finally have a personal site again.

This one's different from the last attempt. No design paralysis, no obsessing over the perfect stack — just a place to put things. Writing, projects, notes on whatever I'm tinkering with.

## What this is

A personal hub. Mostly for myself, but public because:

1. Keeping things public forces some minimum quality bar
2. Someone might find it useful
3. I like reading other people's personal sites and want to contribute to that ecosystem

## What I'll write about

Mostly the intersection of things I actually do:

- Homelab stuff — Proxmox, self-hosting, networking experiments
- Open source — things I'm building or contributing to
- Tooling and workflow — the usual setup-post rabbit hole
- Occasionally something completely unrelated

No promises on frequency. This isn't a content strategy, it's a notebook.

## The stack

SvelteKit with the static adapter, posts in Markdown via mdsvex. Deployed somewhere cheap. Nothing fancy.

Let's see how this goes.
